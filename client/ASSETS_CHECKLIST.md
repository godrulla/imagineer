## 📥 Export Settings

### Logos
- Format: SVG (preferred) or PNG
- Background: Transparent
- Multiple sizes: 16, 32, 64, 128, 256, 512

### Illustrations
- Format: PNG or WebP
- Background: Transparent where applicable
- Size: 2x for retina displays

### Social Images
- Format: JPG
- Size: Exact dimensions (1200x630 for OG)
- Compression: 85% quality
